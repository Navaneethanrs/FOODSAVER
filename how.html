How It Works

Our 4-Step Process

Step 1: Collect Surplus

We gather extra food from restaurants, events, and households that would otherwise go to waste.

Step 2: Safe Transport

Our volunteers transport the food in hygienic containers to maintain freshness and safety.

Step 3: Organize & Store

Collected food is sorted and stored properly to ensure it is ready for distribution.

Step 4: Distribute

We deliver meals directly to homeless shelters, orphanages, and families in need.
